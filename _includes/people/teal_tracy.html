Tracy Teal has a background in bioinformatics and microbial ecology. She is currently an assistant professor in BEACON at Michigan State University and is affiliated with the Enterics Research Investigational Network (ERIN) group and the Institute for Cyber-Enabled Research (iCER). Tracy teaches short courses in microbial metagenomics at the Marine Biological Laboratory and the Kellogg Biological Station. She tweets at @tracykteal.
